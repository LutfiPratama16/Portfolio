Who AM I?

Hi, I’m Lutfi Pratama—a curious mind, a fast learner, and a believer in lifelong learning. I see the web not just as code and layout, but as a canvas to express ideas, solve problems, and connect stories.

Outside of coding, I enjoy reading books that stretch the mind, writing as a way to reflect and explore thoughts, and sketching to visualize ideas. Whether it’s words, lines, or code—I love building things that carry meaning.

Creativity, to me, isn’t limited to one medium. I find joy in turning complexity into clarity, and learning something new every step of the way.
